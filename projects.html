Hour of Code

What happened during the Hour of Code? | Did the participants use similar courses previously? | Did they like it?

On 15th of November, students of ADA University: Nurlan Alakbarli, Rufat Miriyev, Lala Aslanova and Nasib Pashayev hosted the “Hour of Code” event with the DP1 (10th Grade) students at Dunya IB School. Before conducting the lesson, we as a team collaborated numerous times to plan the structure of our upcoming event and distribute the roles among the members of the group. Upon our arrival at Dunya IB School, we decided to split the lesson into four parts so that each team-member would receive an equal opportunity to demonstrate their teaching and assisting abilities.

Our “Hour of Code” lecture commenced with a small peptalk, alongside with a presentation, about ADA University, covering all the advantageous aspects and opportunities ADA students benefit from, while studying there. Shortly after the introduction part, we proceeded by launching the “Classic Maze puzzle” on Code.Org platform. The puzzle consisted of 20 unique missions, where each member demonstrated 5 levels.
